Welcome to the Cosmos

Experience the infinite beauty of space as you journey through this 3D starfield. Notice how the stars move and shift as you scroll, creating a mesmerizing parallax effect that simulates traveling through the depths of space.

The stars dance around you in three-dimensional space, while occasional shooting stars streak across the void, leaving trails of light in their wake.

Stellar Navigation

As you scroll through this cosmic landscape, the stars respond to your movement, creating depth and dimension. The closer stars move faster than the distant ones, just like looking out the window of a spacecraft.

Each star exists in its own layer of space, some near, some far, all contributing to the illusion of traversing the galaxy at incredible speeds.

Shooting Stars

Keep watching for the magical shooting stars that periodically streak across the sky. These celestial visitors appear randomly, each following its own unique path through the starfield.

The motion trails they leave behind fade gently, creating beautiful ephemeral artwork against the cosmic backdrop.

Infinite Journey

This journey through space is endless. The stars continuously regenerate, ensuring that your voyage through the cosmos never ends. Each scroll reveals new depths of space to explore.

The parallax effect creates a sense of speed and movement, making you feel as though you're piloting your own spacecraft through the infinite expanse of the universe.
